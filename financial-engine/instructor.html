What's Included in the Instructor Packet

The comprehensive instructor packet provides all necessary materials to implement this assignment in your entrepreneurship course:

  • Assignment Description: Detailed overview of the Financial Engine assignment and its educational objectives
  • Learning Outcomes: Clear articulation of what students will learn and be able to demonstrate
  • Implementation Guide: Step-by-step instructions for introducing and teaching the assignment
  • Grading Rubric: Detailed rubric for evaluating student work consistently and fairly
  • Answer Keys: Sample solutions and expected outputs for each business archetype
  • Teaching Notes: Tips for common challenges and student questions
  • Assessment Guidelines: Best practices for evaluating financial models

Course Integration Suggestions

The Financial Engine assignment can be integrated into your course in several ways:

  • Standalone Assignment: Use as a comprehensive project covering financial modeling and projections
  • Capstone Project: Incorporate as the financial component of a larger business plan
  • Iterative Learning: Break into multiple assignments building progressively throughout the semester
  • Team Project: Assign as a group project with role specialization
  • Case Study Analysis: Use the archetypes for comparative analysis exercises
